Welcome to Eurotrip!

The objective of the game is to locate ten cities by clicking on the appropriate location on the map. As a visual cue you will be shown three pictures of sights or places in the city.

Points will be calculated depending on the distance of your answer to the actual location of the city. If your answer is in a 200km radius you will be given a bonus question for additional points.

You have the option to load three more pictures for each question, which will be penalized by -200pts. Additionally, you can "purchase" a textual hint for 400pts.

Have fun and happy guessing!
